Privacy Policy

Hush Camera — Last updated: April 5, 2026

Excellcube ("we", "us", or "our") built the Hush app (the "App") as a commercial product. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Hush is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your photos, videos, or any media you capture
  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your contacts, calendars, or browsing history
  • Any biometric data

All photos and videos you take with Hush are stored only on your device (or your personal iCloud Drive if you choose that option). We have no access to your media.

2. Device Permissions

The App requests the following permissions solely to provide its core features:

  • Camera — Required to capture photos and videos.
  • Photo Library — Required to save captured media and display your gallery.
  • Microphone — Optional. Used only during video recording to include audio. If denied, videos are recorded without sound.
  • Location — Optional (Pro feature). Used only to embed GPS coordinates in photo EXIF metadata when you enable this setting. Location data is stored only in the photo file on your device; it is never sent to us.

3. Advertising

The free version of Hush displays advertisements provided by AppLovin MAX, a third-party advertising service. To deliver relevant ads, AppLovin may collect certain device-level information such as:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA), if you grant tracking permission
  • Device model, OS version, and language
  • General (non-precise) location based on IP address
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by AppLovin under their own privacy policy. You can review it at applovin.com/privacy.

If you decline the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt, the IDFA will not be shared with ad networks, and you will see non-personalized ads.

Hush Pro users enjoy a completely ad-free experience with no advertising-related data collection.

4. In-App Purchases

Hush offers a one-time "Pro" purchase processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information. Purchase status is synced via iCloud Key-Value Storage to restore your purchase across your devices.

5. iCloud

If you choose to save photos to iCloud Drive, files are stored in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to your iCloud data. iCloud data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

6. Analytics

Hush does not use any analytics SDKs or tracking tools. We do not collect usage statistics, crash reports, or behavioral data through third-party services.

7. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store your personal data on any server, there is no risk of a data breach on our end. All your data remains on your device under the protection of iOS security features, including device encryption and app sandboxing.

8. Children's Privacy

Hush does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Advertisements shown in the free version are limited to family-safe content (max ad content rating: G).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
