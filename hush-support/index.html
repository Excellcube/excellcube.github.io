Frequently Asked Questions

How does silent capture work?

Hush captures still images from the camera's live video data stream instead of using the system's standard shutter action. This allows photos and videos to be recorded without any shutter sound, including the start and stop recording tones.

Why does Hush ask for microphone permission?

The microphone permission is only requested when you start recording a video. It is used exclusively to include audio in your video recordings. If you deny the permission, videos will be recorded without sound. Hush never accesses the microphone outside of active video recording.

Why does Hush ask for location permission?

Location is optional and turned off by default. If you enable GPS EXIF tagging in Settings (a Pro feature), Hush will embed the GPS coordinates into the photo's EXIF metadata. Location data is stored only in the photo file on your device and is never sent to our servers.

How do I save photos to iCloud Drive automatically?

Open Hush → Settings → Storage → iCloud Drive, and toggle it on. You will be asked to select a folder in your iCloud Drive where captured photos should be saved. From then on, every photo you capture is automatically copied to that folder. You can turn this off at any time.

The volume button shutter doesn't work. What should I do?

Make sure Hush is in the foreground and the camera view is visible. The volume button shutter only works while the camera screen is active. If the issue persists, check that Hush has camera permission in the iOS Settings app under Privacy & Security → Camera.

How do I restore my Hush Pro purchase on a new device?

Open Hush → Settings → Hush Pro → "Restore Purchases". Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to buy Pro. The purchase will be restored within a few seconds at no additional cost.

How do I request a refund?

All in-app purchases are handled by Apple, so refund requests must go through Apple directly. Please visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and select the Hush Pro purchase to request a refund.

Does Hush run in the background?

No. Hush releases the camera session immediately when you leave the app. It does not run in the background, does not track your location when closed, and does not consume battery when not in use. This is a core design principle of the app.

Why is my photo saved as HEIF instead of JPEG?

Hush defaults to HEIF for smaller file sizes on supported devices. To switch to JPEG, open Hush → Settings → Format → JPEG. HEIF offers better compression, while JPEG provides wider compatibility when sharing with other apps or services.

Is it legal to use Hush where I live?

Hush is designed for respectful photography in quiet environments such as libraries, classrooms, and meeting rooms. You are responsible for using the app in accordance with local laws regarding photography, recording, and privacy. Please do not use Hush to photograph or record others without their consent where such consent is required. See our Terms of Service for details.
